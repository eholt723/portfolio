About

I am a software engineer with a strong focus on AI and machine learning, specializing in building and deploying production-ready systems. My work spans applied ML projects such as Transport-LLM, an in-browser language model for transportation systems, and WarehouseBot-RL, a reinforcement learning agent for logistics optimization, as well as full-stack SaaS platforms supporting real users, authentication, and subscription-based access.

Currently completing my B.S. in Computer Information Systems (Software Programming specialization), I am developing deep expertise in Python, PyTorch, and scikit-learn, with a focus on applied AI, system design, and production deployment.
